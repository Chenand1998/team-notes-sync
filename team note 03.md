### **The Accidental Public Note**

One day, Mike was using HackMD to write his _super secret diet plan_. It included lines like “Only eat one slice of cake a day” and “Visit the convenience store at 11 PM for snacks.”

Unfortunately, he accidentally made the note **public**—and even shared the link in his team chat.

Minutes later, teammates started commenting:  
“This diet I can follow!”  
“Is this a reverse diet? 😂”

Mike was speechless. He quickly renamed the note to “Happy Food Diary.”  
Surprisingly, it became the **most popular note** on the team.
